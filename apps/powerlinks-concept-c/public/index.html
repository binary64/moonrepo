New Chapter

SAME CRAFT.
FRESH VISION. Seven motorcycle shops in the area have closed. Powerlinks is still here — and it's just getting started.

Powerlinks has been the go-to motorcycle shop on Hightown Industrial Estate for years. While the competition closed their doors, we kept ours open — because this community needs a bike shop it can trust.

From April 2026, Sam Thompson takes the helm. New energy, new ideas, same dedication to the craft. Whether you need a chain kit for your commuter, an MOT for your weekend toy, or advice on the best tyres for a touring trip — this is your place.

We're not a franchise. We're not a warehouse. We're the bloke who'll recognise your bike in the car park and remember what sprockets you need.
