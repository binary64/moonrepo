Our Story

Been here since before you had your licence.

Powerlinks has been supplying parts and servicing motorcycles from our unit on Hightown Industrial Estate in Ringwood for years. We've watched the other shops close — seven in the surrounding area — and we're still here.

That's not luck. It's because we know bikes, we know our customers, and we don't mess about. If the part you need isn't in stock, we'll source it fast and at a price that won't make you wince. Our advice is always free — because that's how it should be.

Under new ownership from April 2026, Sam Thompson is carrying the torch. Same workshop. Same commitment. Same phone number you've been calling for years.
